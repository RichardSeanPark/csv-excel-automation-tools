Bank Statement CSV Cleanup Checklist

Before using a bank statement CSV in a report, import, or spreadsheet analysis, check the structure first.

Checklist

  • Dates are in one consistent format.
  • Debit and credit values are not mixed into the description column.
  • Negative and positive amounts are consistent.
  • Balance values are numeric.
  • Header and footer rows are removed.
  • Rejected or unclear rows are reviewed manually.

What You Want

The goal is not just to create an Excel file. The goal is to create a file you can review, import, or summarize without hidden row-level mistakes.
